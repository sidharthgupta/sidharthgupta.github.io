Sidharth Gupta

I have been a research assistant and PhD candidate in the Electrical and Computer Engineering at the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign (UIUC) since 2017. I am advised by Ivan Dokmanić and we are affiliated with the Coordinated Science Laboratory. I am interested in developing machine learning data-driven methods for solving imaging problems. This work falls under the broad umbrellas of signal processing and machine learning.

I graduated from Churchill College at the University of Cambridge with bachelor's and master's degrees in Engineering in 2014. Between Cambridge and UIUC I worked at Samsung Research in South Korea where I designed new APIs for smartphones, smart TV, smartwatches and other IoT devices. I used to regularly speak about this work in conferences sessions, keynotes, online shows and other online tutorials.
